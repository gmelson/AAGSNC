The Perfect Cup

 

The team at Weekend Coffee Roasters take pride in making the perfect cup of coffee...especially coffee snob co-owner Greg. We found that following NCA (National Coffee Association) and Eating Well Magazine steps for brewing the perfect cup complements and enhances Weekend coffee. To brew a perfect cup of coffee at home please follow the instructions below.

  1. Buy Fresh Beans
  2. Keep Coffee Beans Fresh by storing it in airtight containers
  3. Choose Good Coffee
  4. Grind Your Own Beans
  5. Use Good Water
  6. Avoid Cheap Filters
  7. Don't Skimp On The Coffee - The standard measure for brewing coffee of proper strength is 2 level tablespoons per 6-ounce cup or about 2 3/4 tablespoons per 8-ounce cup. Tricks like using less coffee and hotter water to extract more cups per pound tend to make for bitter brews.
  8. Watch The Heat Water that is too hot will extract compounds in the coffee that are bitter rather than pleasant. The proper brewing temperature is 200F, or about 45 seconds off a full boil. (Most good coffeemakers regulate this automatically.)
  9. Keep Your Equipment Clean

For more detailed instructions please see the links to: Eating Well http://mobile.eatingwell.com/healthy_cooking/healthy_cooking_101_basics_and_techniques/9_rules_for_how_to_make_a_perfect_cup_of_coffee?page=1 And NCA (National Coffee Association) http://www.ncausa.org/i4a/pages/index.cfm?pageID=71
